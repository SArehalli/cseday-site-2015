What is CSE Day?

CSE day is a signature event for both the CSE Department and the Computer Science and Engineering Society. It is a day dedicated to both fueling the passion and education of students interested in Computer Science and Engineering and highlighting the broad opportunities available to those students, be it in industry or academia. Our goal is to show students the different paths available to them and teach them how to navigate those paths based on real world experience.

The event allows interested UCSD students to talk with recruiters, learn the secrets of the technical interview, see the cutting edge of the field, and, most importantly, have a glimpse into what may potentially be their future.

Mark your calendars for Thursday, January 22nd, 2015, and check the schedule below for more info about the events planned. Hope to see you there!
